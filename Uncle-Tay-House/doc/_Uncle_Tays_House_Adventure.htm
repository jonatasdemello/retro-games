About the Story:
In this adventure you find treasures and valuables in your mad uncle Tays' house type simple commands: north, south, etc.
To move (or just 'n', 's'). Take and drop, inventory, look, read, move, and so on.
Some commands are complex: 'move the hubcap with the spanner'.
